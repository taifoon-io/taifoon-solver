PHASE 01 — DETECT

Mempool monitor watches every bridge.

The mempool-monitor crate ingests intent feeds from 10 bridge protocols in parallel — Across V3, Mayan Swift, LayerZero V2, Stargate V2, LI.FI V2, Squid Router, Hop, Orbiter, Hyperlane, and t3rn LWC. Every signed order surfaces here within one block of the source chain.

// fixtures/across_v3_intents.json — 1 of 10 sources
intent 0xfA12...3D9 · base→arb · 5,000 USDC
intent 0x71B4...8eF · opt→base · 0.31 ETH
intent 0x9CD2...4A1 · sol→eth · 100 SOL [mayan]
PHASE 02 — SCORE

Profit-calc rejects unfillable orders.

profit-calc + portfolio-sidecar evaluate each intent against live wallet balances, gas oracles, and slippage curves. Unrealistic profits and gas-griefing patterns are filtered (see fixtures/unrealistic_profits.json).

PHASE 03 — QUOTE

Protocol adapters compose the route.

protocol-adapters and protocol-adapters-solana materialize the calldata for every supported venue. Solana-specific routes use Mayan Swift, Jupiter, and Drift; EVM uses Across, Stargate, LI.FI. The genome-client picks the optimal split.

// winning split for SOL → USDC.e on Arbitrum
mayan-swift SOL → USDC @solana
across-v3 USDC → USDC.e @arbitrum
└ quoted: +0.22% net of gas
PHASE 04 — FILL

Wallet-manager broadcasts under deadline.

The solver-main loop signs and broadcasts atomically. Failures revert the entire bundle; partial fills are impossible. Deadline-driven — if the solver can't beat the timestamp, it abstains and the order returns to the auction.

PHASE 05 — SETTLE

SuperRoot anchors the proof.

Every fill emits an inclusion proof under Taifoon's SuperRoot — the same MMR-attested L1→L5 system used by Pulse, Search, and Proof. Cross-chain settlement is verifiable from any chain to any chain. 0 bridge trust.
